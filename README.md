## Overview

PixelPolice is a lightweight and fast **image moderation microservice**. It is designed to help platforms, social apps keep images safe and compliant.

Features include:

- **CLIP-based detection** of banned symbols and logos  
- **OCR scanning** for text and emojis on images  
- **Object Detection** to identify small or hidden elements  
- **FastAPI backend**, easily integrable with Django REST, React, or other frontends
